Transform Your Organization's AI Learning

A comprehensive, ready-to-deploy AI learning program designed for people with no prior AI experience. Help your team confidently navigate the AI revolution with our proven 10-challenge learning journey.

Low stakes. Low risk. Privacy-first design. No data collection, no sign-ins, no tracking. We've created 10 unique challenges that take your team from AI novice to confident user through hands-on, practical activities.

Ready to deploy today. Complete program with all materials, instructions, and support resources included. Fully customizable to match your organization's branding and specific needs.

We have identified four stages of AI use that we think are important as we start to explore AI:



Recognize:

Identify where AI exists in your world and how it can be useful

Investigate:

Explore AI tools and their potential

Supercharge:

Leverage AI to enhance creativity, productivity, and decision-making potentiating your actions

Exchange:

Collaborate with AI in a dynamic, interactive way, blending human expertise with AI's capabilities
